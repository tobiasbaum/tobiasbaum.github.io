Online Card Sorting Tool

Card Sorting is a technique to bring order into a set of items by assigning them to buckets one after the other. You can use it for research, development, or just to structure your thoughts. Card sorting is usually performed with snippets of paper, but this online tool allows you to do card sorting in the browser. Just enter the items below and click start.
